Bike Walk Vote Holds Endorsement Announcement and Campaign Kick-Off Event: 4/4 Forward

Portland, March 2012 – Bike Walk Vote, the political arm of the area’s biking, walking, and transit community meets tonight to connect Bike Walk Vote activists and our endorsed candidates in order to make personal connections and discuss how together we can move the campaigns forward and get our candidates elected.

Bike Walk Vote’s 4/4 Forward event will be held tonight, Wednesday, April 4th from 7:00p.m. – 9:00p.m. at Crank Bicycles on SE 27th and Ash.

Each of our endorsed candidates will be in attendance including Portland Mayoral candidate Jefferson Smith, Portland City Council candidates Mary Nolan and Steve Novick, Metro Council candidates Sam Chase, Craig Dirksen, and Bob Stacey, and Washington County Commission candidate Dick Schouten.

Tonight’s event is all-ages-friendly, admission is free, as are light snacks and refreshments, thanks to generous donations from Crema Bakery, Hotlips Pizza, New Belgium Brewing and Ninkasi Brewing.

At our December 4th, 2011 kickoff party Bike Walk Vote attracted over 100 active transportation activists and was co-emceed by Congressman Earl Blumenauer as well as Multnomah County Commission Chair Jeff Cogen. Visuals from the event are available on our Facebook page with credit to www.bikewalkvote.org.
